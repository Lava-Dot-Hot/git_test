Steam out the bitterness

Apparently the bitter taste comes from amonia which is a by product
of the fermentation process. Steaming will remove this unpleasant
taste as well as softening up the tempeh. This will help it to soak
in the marinade. I will be steaming my tempeh in a rice cooker. The
internet reccomends 10 - 15 minutes.

The Marinade

We've all mixed some stuff together before. Right? Throw all the stuff
listed below into a sealable container and mix it up. Then put the steamed
tempeh inside and put it in the refridgerator for 24 hours. Flip halfway through.

  • Tamari
  • Liquid smoke
  • Smoked paprika
  • Black pepper
  • Maple syrup
  • Vegetable bouillon

Bake and Baste

Preheat the oven to 425. Take the tempeh out and save the rest of the marinade.
Pop it in the oven on a baking sheet for 10 minutes. Then put the rest of the
marinade over the tempeh and let it cook for another 10 minutes until golden.
And that should be it. Throw them slices over some rice and enjoy.
